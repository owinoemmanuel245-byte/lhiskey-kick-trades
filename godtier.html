Quick Setup Guide

1
Download MetaTrader 5 and open a broker account
▼
  1. Download MetaTrader 5 from your broker's website (e.g. HFM, IC Markets, Exness).
  2. Create a live or demo account with your broker.
  3. Log in to MT5 using your account number, password, and server name.
  4. Make sure your broker supports US500, US100, US30, and XAUUSD as tradable symbols.
2
Install the GodTier Zero 1 Expert Advisor in MT5
▼
  1. Download the GodTier Zero 1 EA (.ex5) file above.
  2. In MT5, go to File → Open Data Folder.
  3. Navigate to MQL5 → Experts.
  4. Copy the .ex5 file into that folder.
  5. Restart MT5 or press F5 in the Navigator panel.
  6. The EA will appear under Expert Advisors in the Navigator.
3
Activate your license key in the EA
▼
  1. Drag the EA onto any chart (e.g. XAUUSD M5).
  2. In the EA settings dialog, paste your license key: —
  3. Enable Allow Algo Trading and Allow WebRequests.
  4. Add lhiskeykicktrades.vercel.app to MT5's allowed URLs list:
    Tools → Options → Expert Advisors → Allow WebRequests
  5. Click OK. The EA will contact the license server and activate.
  6. Your device will be automatically bound — this counts as your one device lock.
4
Configure risk settings and select your bot mode
▼
  • Conservative: Stricter filters, lower risk (0.5%). Best for funded/prop accounts or beginners.
  • Standard: Balanced — default mode for most users (0.5%–1%).
  • Aggressive: More trades, slightly lower score threshold. Accept extra risk warning.
  • Prop Firm: Strictest drawdown protection. Score 80+ required. Lower risk.
  • Demo: No real execution. Logs signals only. Use for testing before going live.

Start with Demo or Conservative mode first. Forward-test for at least 2 weeks before using Standard or Aggressive mode with real funds.

5
Understanding sessions — when the bot trades
▼

The bot only trades during two windows:

  • London Open: First 60–120 minutes after London market opens (~08:00 UTC / 11:00 EAT)
  • New York Open: First 60–120 minutes after NY market opens (~13:30 UTC / 16:30 EAT)

Outside these windows the bot watches but does not trade. This is intentional — quality over quantity. Do not expect trades every hour.

MT5 must be running and connected during session windows for the bot to execute.

6
XAUUSD (Gold) — special rules you must understand
▼

Gold is filtered differently from indices. The bot requires:

  • A liquidity sweep below/above the previous 15M candle before entry.
  • Strong volume confirmation after the sweep.
  • A minimum GodTier Decision Score of 75+ (80+ in Prop Firm mode).
  • Stricter spread and news filters.

This means Gold will trade less frequently than indices. That is by design — Gold fakeouts are very common and the bot protects you from them.

7
Renewing your subscription
▼
  1. Go to the Payments page on the main website.
  2. Send your M-Pesa payment and include your license key as the reference.
  3. Your subscription will be extended by 30 days within 24 hours (usually faster).
  4. The bot will resume automatically — no reinstall needed.

When your subscription expires, the bot stops opening new trades but manages any already-open trades safely.

8
Switching to a new device
▼

Each license is locked to one device at a time. If you get a new PC or reinstall MT5:

  1. Contact support on WhatsApp with your license key and reason.
  2. Admin will reset your device lock within 24 hours.
  3. Activate on your new device using the same license key.

Do not share your license key. Sharing triggers the device lock and your access will be suspended.
